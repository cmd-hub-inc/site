# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be a welcoming, respectful, and productive community for everyone. We pledge to act and communicate in ways that contribute to an open, inclusive, and harassment-free environment.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed by the project — issues, pull requests, project discussions, chat channels, mailing lists, and in-person events organized by the project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in interactions.
- Use inclusive language and assume good faith.
- Be open to constructive feedback and give it kindly.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include, but are not limited to:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form.
- Derogatory or demeaning comments about a person or group.
- Posting sexually explicit content or violent threats.
- Insulting, demeaning, or exclusionary language.
- Sustained disruption of project activities or conversations.

## Reporting and Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainers so it can be addressed promptly. You can report by opening an issue titled "Code of Conduct Incident" or by contacting the maintainers directly through the repository's contact method.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated fairly and promptly by the maintainers. When a report is found to violate this Code of Conduct, maintainers may take actions including private warnings, temporary or permanent removal from project spaces, or other measures deemed appropriate.

## Scope of Remedies

Remedial actions will be proportionate to the severity and frequency of the violation, and may consider the impact on the community and the safety of its members.

## Attribution

This is a project-specific Code of Conduct intended as a clear, concise statement of expected behavior. Maintainters may adapt it to fit this project's needs.
